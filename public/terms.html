2. Service description

TeacherAI is an AI-assisted lesson planning platform designed for Ontario elementary teachers. The service includes:

  • AI-generated lesson plans, student worksheets, reading resources, and assessment rubrics aligned to the Ontario curriculum

  • Assessment tracking, grade tracking, and descriptive feedback generation
