1. Who we are

TeacherAI (teacherai.ca) is an AI-assisted lesson planning tool built for Ontario elementary teachers, with a focus on SSC (Self-Contained Special Education) and split-grade classrooms. TeacherAI is operated by an individual Ontario teacher and is not affiliated with any school board.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use TeacherAI.

3. Student data

⚠️
MFIPPA notice: Student data collected through TeacherAI may be subject to the Municipal Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (MFIPPA) in Ontario. We recommend using first names only or pseudonyms until your school board formally approves this tool. Do not upload full IEP documents.

TeacherAI collects minimal student information — first names, grade level, and teacher-written accommodation notes. This is limited to what a teacher would write in a lesson plan or class list.

What we do not collect:

  • Student email addresses or passwords (students have no accounts)
  • Student photos or biometric data
  • Psychological assessments or full IEP documents
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Student Ontario Education Numbers (OEN)

Students access worksheets through a shared link — they type their first name to identify themselves. No student account is created.

4. How AI is used

TeacherAI uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate lesson plans, worksheets, rubrics, and report card comments. When content is generated:

  • Student names are never sent to Anthropic. Students are anonymized as "Student A, Student B" etc. before any AI call.
  • Learning profiles are anonymized. Modified expectations are sent with anonymized labels only.
  • Assessment data is never sent to Anthropic. Marks and grades stay on Canadian servers only.
  • Worksheet submissions are never sent to Anthropic. Student answers stay in our database only.

What IS sent to Anthropic: topic, grade level, subject, duration, anonymized learning needs, and class context notes. Anthropic's API does not use API data to train models by default. For details, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Data storage & residency

All data is stored in Canada. We use Supabase with the Canada Central (ca-central-1) region, hosted on AWS infrastructure in Montreal, Quebec.

This means your data — including student names, assessment records, and lesson content — never leaves Canada at rest. Canadian data residency helps support compliance with PIPEDA and MFIPPA requirements for Ontario educators.

Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's storage layer.

7. Retention & deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Lessons and worksheets: Retained indefinitely until you delete them or your account
  • Worksheet submissions: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Assessment data: Retained until you delete sessions or your account
  • Student roster: Retained until you remove students or your account

You can delete individual students, lessons, and assessment sessions directly from the app. To request full account deletion and erasure of all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Future update: We plan to add an in-app account deletion feature and automatic data expiry for worksheet submissions older than one school year.

8. Your rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable Ontario privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data (CSV export available in the Grade Tracker)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

9. MFIPPA & Ontario schools

TeacherAI is designed with Ontario's privacy landscape in mind, including the Municipal Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (MFIPPA), which governs student data in Ontario school boards.

Recommendations for Ontario teachers:

  • Use first names only (not full names) in the class roster during the feedback period
  • Do not paste full IEP documents — use your own professional notes summarizing modified expectations only
  • Check your board's acceptable use policy before entering student data into any third-party tool
  • If your board requires a Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) before approving tools, contact us — we can provide documentation to support that process

TeacherAI is not currently approved by any Ontario school board. Individual teachers use this tool under their own professional judgment. We are working toward formal board approval processes for Phase 3 of the product.
